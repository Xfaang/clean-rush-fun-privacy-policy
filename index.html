Overview

CleanRush is a desktop organization tool for macOS that helps you sort and organize files on your desktop in a fun, gamified way. This privacy policy explains how CleanRush handles your data.

Data Collection

CleanRush does NOT collect any personal data. The app operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

Local Data Storage

CleanRush stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Statistics: Points earned, items sorted, achievements unlocked, and session information
  • Configuration: Your folder preferences and keyboard shortcuts
  • Activity Log: History of file organization actions for undo functionality

All data is stored in your Documents folder under ~/Documents/CleanRush/ and never leaves your device.

File Access

CleanRush requires access to your Desktop folder to function properly. This access is used exclusively to:

  • Scan for files and folders that need organization
  • Move files to destination folders you specify
  • Create new organization folders as needed

The app only accesses files you explicitly choose to organize and does not read file contents or access files outside your Desktop folder without permission.

Third-Party Services

CleanRush does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The app operates completely offline and independently.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your local device, your privacy and security are maintained by your macOS system's built-in security features. CleanRush operates within macOS security sandbox requirements.

Children's Privacy

CleanRush is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
